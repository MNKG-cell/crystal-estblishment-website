UNDERCOAT WHITE

An Undercoat is used after primer.
It is usuallu used to fill any minor imperfections to create a smooth, even-coloured surface, ready for application of the topcoat.
An undercoat also assists to lighten a surface when changing from a dark to pale color.
It is used as an intermediate coat as part of the alkyd coating system


Product Description and use

Crystal paint Undercoat white is a professionally
quality, solvent-based undercoat suitable for use on all types of interior
and exterior wood, metal and suitably primed masonry surfaces.
