NAME Silk Vinyl Emulsion
MAJOR USE Silk Vinyl Emulsion gives a high sheen
finish and is the most
durable of all the emulsion paints.
It is good for rooms that are
subject to alot of moisture. ie, condensation.
We make specific kitchen and bathroom
paint which is ideal for areas of humidity.
KEY BENEFITS
  • Tough, Washable Finish
  • High Opacity
  • Coverage up to 16m^2 per litre
COMPOSITION
  • Pigment: Lightfast pigments
  • PVA Copolymer Emulsion
  • Solvent: Water
TYPICAL USE Suitable for interior use on all normal interior wall and ceiling surfaces. For interior surfaces that are likely to suffer from mould growth, use mould shield Fungicidal Eggshell.
SURFACE PREPARATION To get the best results with crystal paint silk vinyl, make sure surfaces are painted sound, clean,dry. new surfaces in particular must be dry.
