OVERVIEW.

CRYSTAL ESTABLISHMENTS LIMITED was started in the year of 2012.
It was started by , Mr Agaba Edson who was inspired by his Late Father, Mr.Katwiire.

He was open minded and his main aim was to create a unique, orignal and special type of paint. He went ahead and asked a great Chemist, who led him through the process of mixing different chemicals inorder to make the most durable type of paint in Uganda. He started off by producing Undercoat white paint, which was only packed in 20L jerrycans and 5L jerrycans by then.

With time, there was more demand of different sizes, so he packed the paint in 4L tins, 1L tins and 20L buckets.
